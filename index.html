About

Two generations of
precision manufacturing.

Founded in 1977 by Mike Watts Sr. in Eastlake, Ohio, Astro Manufacturing has grown from a small machine shop into a 197,000 square foot, full-service contract manufacturer serving the most demanding industries in the world.

Under the leadership of Mike Watts Jr. since 1999, the company has earned NADCAP accreditation and ISO certification — delivering aerospace, defense, and medical components with the quality these industries require.
